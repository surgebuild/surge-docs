In **Bitcoin L2's**, A typical transaction flow within this architecture begins at the
application layer, moves through L2 or L3 solutions for processing, and concludes
with final settlement on the Bitcoin blockchain. This streamlined process ensures
efficiency, security, and adherence to Bitcoin's decentralized nature.
